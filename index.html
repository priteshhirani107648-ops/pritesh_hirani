Knowledge_Base

> Accessing Physical Design Core Memory...

Setup Time

Static Timing Analysis

Setup Time

The minimum amount of time the data signal must be stable before the active edge of the clock arrives at the flip-flop.

Hold Time

Static Timing Analysis

Hold Time

The minimum amount of time the data signal must remain stable after the active edge of the clock arrives.

CTS

Clock Tree Synthesis

CTS

The process of distributing the clock signal to all sequential elements with minimum skew and insertion delay to ensure synchronous operation.

IR Drop

Power Integrity

IR Drop

The voltage drop across the power and ground mesh due to the resistance (R) of the metal and the current (I) drawn by the standard cells.

DRC

Physical Signoff

Design Rule Check

Verification that the layout meets the foundry's physical manufacturing constraints, such as minimum width, spacing, and area.

Decap Cells

Power Management

Decap Cells

Decoupling capacitors placed near high-switching active cells to provide instantaneous current and mitigate dynamic IR drop.
